# Community Witnesses – Website Specification (Gemini.md)

## Project Overview

Community Witnesses is a purpose-driven organisation dedicated to restoring hope, dignity, and transformation within communities affected by homelessness, addiction, mental health challenges, and social deprivation.

This website will serve as a digital hub to inform, inspire, and engage supporters, volunteers, and beneficiaries.

---

## Brand Tone & Voice

* Compassionate and empathetic
* Hopeful and uplifting
* Faith-inspired but inclusive
* Clear, simple UK English
* Non-judgemental and respectful

---

## Vision Statement

We envision a community where individuals and families at risk of, or affected by, homelessness, drug addiction, mental health challenges, and other social issues are treated with love, respect, and dignity, and are fully supported towards lasting freedom and restoration.

---

## Mission Statement

Community Witnesses exists to restore hope and dignity to members of our community who are at risk, or already experiencing hardship and deprivation.

We demonstrate love, the power of faith for transformation, and service to God.

We help individuals gain victory, rediscover themselves, create positive change, and build a stronger, healthier community where everyone matters.

---

## Core Values

### Love

We are committed to demonstrating genuine love within our community.
We believe love is the solution to social challenges and must be expressed without judgement or condemnation.

### Hope

We never give up on anyone.
We believe that with the right support today, a brighter future is possible for every individual and the wider community.

### Faith

We believe in the power of God for transformation and restoration.
We rely on Him for guidance and for complete freedom from life’s struggles.

### Respect

We believe in treating everyone with respect, especially those we serve.
We create a safe and inclusive environment where everyone feels valued and connected.

### Dignity

We uphold the dignity of every individual, regardless of class, ethnicity, or religion.
Everyone deserves to be treated with honour and worth.

### Integrity

We are honest and transparent in all we do.
We say what we mean and stand by our words.

### Accountability

We are accountable to our stakeholders, partners, and the communities we serve.

## Website Structure

### 1. Home Page

* Hero section with strong message (Hope, Restoration, Community)
* Brief introduction to Community Witnesses
* Call-to-action buttons:

  * Donate
  * Get Involved
* Highlights of impact/services

---

### 2. About Us

* Vision Statement
* Mission Statement
* Core Values
* Brief history (optional)

---

### 3. Our Work / Services

* Support for homelessness
* Addiction recovery support
* Mental health support
* Community outreach programmes

---

### 4. Donate

**Objective:** Enable secure and simple giving.

**Requirements:**

* Payment link integration (e.g., Stripe, Paystack, Flutterwave)
* Options:

  * One-time donation
  * Recurring donation (optional)
* Thank-you confirmation message

**CTA Copy Example:**
“Your support restores hope. Every contribution makes a difference.”

---

### 5. Get Involved

**Volunteer Sign-Up Form**

**Form Fields:**

* Full Name
* Email Address
* Phone Number
* Area of Interest (Dropdown)

  * Volunteering
  * Outreach
  * Admin Support
  * Other
* Availability
* Message / Why you want to join

**CTA:**
“Be part of the change in your community.”

---

### 6. Contact Us

**Content:**

* Official email address (domain-based)
* Phone number
* Social media links (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, etc.)

**Optional:**

* Contact form
* Google Map (if location is available)

---

## Design Guidelines

### Colours

* Primary: Gold / Warm tone (hope, value)
* Secondary: Black or Deep Grey (strength, stability)
* Accent: Soft neutrals (clean and calm look)

### Typography

* Clean and readable (e.g., Inter, Open Sans, or Verdana)
* Clear hierarchy (Headings, Subheadings, Body text)

### Layout

* Minimal and modern
* Mobile-first responsive design
* Clear spacing and readability
* Accessible (high contrast, simple navigation)

---

## Technical Stack (Recommended)

* Frontend: React + Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui
* Backend: Django + Django REST Framework
* Payment Integration: Paystack / Stripe
* Hosting: Vercel (frontend) + Render/Heroku (backend)

---

## Key Features

* Fully responsive design
* Clean navigation (top navbar only)
* Secure donation system
* Volunteer onboarding form
* Social media integration
* Fast loading performance
